Portrait Sessions

All sessions include a private gallery, refreshments, and a free 30-minute planning session. The gallery offers an option to purchase metal, acrylic, and traditional prints.

Outdoor sessions will be scheduled in the morning or late afternoon for optimum lighting. In the case of cold/wet weather, I will reschedule at no extra cost.

Let's get creative.

Custom Session

Do you have a specific request for the length of your session, the number of photos, etc.? Reach out to me and I will work with you!

The Mini

A short, 45-minute session perfect for any type of session. You will be able to go to at most 2 locations.

Prices start at $60 per session.

The Grad

A 90-minute session geared towards high school, college, and professional school graduates. This allows for the most "bang-for-your-buck" and gives you plenty of time for your favorite 4 locations.

Prices start at $120 per session. Contact for group pricing.

The Talent

A 2-hour session aimed for one or two individuals, but a group is also acceptable. Great for any kind of passion you have, whehter it be sports, music, or another activity. You will receive 25 pictures, and you'll be able to do 4 locations if you'd like!

Prices start at $200 per session. Contact for group pricing.

The Occasion

Events include festivals, parties, and weddings.

Contact for pricing and number of images.
