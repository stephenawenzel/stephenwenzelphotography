What is included with the session?

A short meeting to prepare you for the session, a beverage of your choice, and resources to help us find what you are looking for.

Final images are distributed within a week after the session in a password-protected gallery with an option to purchase metallic, acrylic, and traditional prints!

What does the process include?

I make everything as efficient, timely, and user-friendly as possible.

Once you fill out this contact form I will respond within 24 hours.

Then, you will receive resources to help you prepare for your session in a custom Google Drive folder.

In the meantime before your session, we will talk about what you would like from your session.

Then it's time for your session!

Within a day or two, I will send you the images to select from, then I will get started on editing them.

Finally, you will receive your pictures in a private gallery in less than a week.
