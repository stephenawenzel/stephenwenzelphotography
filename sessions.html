Custom Session

Do you have a specific request for the length of your session or the number of images? Reach out to me and I will work with you!

The Mini

A short, 45-minute session perfect for any type of session. You can choose up to 2 locations. This is designed for those who need an extra short session such as for business headshots. You can also add this to the end of any other session you sign up for. You will recieve 10-12 images.

Prices start at $60 per session.

The Grad

A 90-minute session geared towards high school, college, and professional school graduates. You will be able to visit 4 of your favorite campus locations, or any location that was especially meaningful to your experience! You will recieve 25-30 images.

Prices start at $120 per session. I will be more than happy to do group photos! The max price will be $200 per session for group photos, which your group can split amongst each other.

Contact

The Talent

A 2-hour session aimed for one or two individuals, but a group is also acceptable. Great for any kind of passion you have, whether it's sports, music, or another activity. You will receive 25-30 pictures, and you have the option to move to up to 4 different locations.

Prices start at $200 per session. Please contact me if you are interested in group pricing. In general, the session will become less expensive for each person as the group gets larger!

Contact

The Occasion

What king of occasion are you throwing? I've photographed everything from weddings, clubs, parties, and graduations. I'm always down for a party!

Pricing for this type of photoshoot varies depending on your interest. You will recieve anywhere from 25 to 35 images per hour depending on the type of gathering.
