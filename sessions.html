What is included?

All sessions include a 20-minute preparation meeting, a non-alcoholic beverage of your choice, and a maximum of 3 locations of your choosing.

Selects are distributed in 3 days or less, and edited pictures are returned after a week, guaranteed.

There is also an option to purchase from metal, acrylic, and traditional prints.

What is the process like?

Once you send in your request, I will get back to you in less than 24 hours.

Then, we will schedule your preparation meeting so we can talk about what you would like based on my portfolio.

Then we will meet at the locations you picked for your session!

After your session, I will send you all the images to choose your selects from. Once you choose them, I will get them edited and distributed to you in a customized, private gallery in less than 3 days after your session.
