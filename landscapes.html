welcome

I'm Stephen Wenzel, a resident of Clyde, NC and a student at UNC Chapel Hill. I first started photography because I was curious about what kinds of animals and plants were found on my family's property. I found all kinds of cool birds and colorful plants, but I wanted to explore even more. That curiosity drove me to begin landscape photography back in 2015, when I was a freshman in high school. Since then, I started portrait photography, incorporating aspects of nature photography into my work.

I have always been interested in celebrating diversity around the world. That's why I am donating 15% of all proceeds annually to Folkmoot, an organization that fosters the vibrancy of many cultures into one community. Folkmoot programs are based on cultural exchange and designed to build global relationships, foster cultural understanding and develop community prosperity. They host a yearly international dance festival with countries representing their culture through their folk dances.
